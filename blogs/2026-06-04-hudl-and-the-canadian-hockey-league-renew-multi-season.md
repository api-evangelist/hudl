Hudl has renewed its scouting rights agreement with the Canadian Hockey League, continuing to provide coverage of three major junior hockey leagues: the WHL, OHL, and QMJHL. Video and data from these competitions will remain available through Hudl Instat for professional scouting and coaching purposes. The partnership reinforces Hudl's commitment to delivering hockey content to its global user base.
