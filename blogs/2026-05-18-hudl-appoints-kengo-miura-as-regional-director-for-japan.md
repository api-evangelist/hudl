Hudl Appoints Kengo Miura as Regional Director for Japan Tokyo, Japan / Lincoln, Nebraska — May 18, 2026 — Hudl, the global leader in sports performance technology, today announced the appointment of Kengo Miura as Regional Director for Japan, effective 18 May. In this role, Kengo will be responsible for leading Hudl’s commercial development and partnership activity across the Japanese market, working with Tim Rouse, Sr. Director Sales - APAC. Tim Rouse, Sr. Director Sales - APAC, said: “Japan is an important and growing market for Hudl. Sports organizations across the country are increasingly looking for new insights, driven by integrated, data tools that help coaches and analysts perform at their best. Kengo’s appointment gives us dedicated local expertise to serve those organizations more effectively, and we look forward to seeing what he builds here.” Kengo Miura said: “I’m delighted to be joining Hudl at such an exciting time for sports technology in Japan. The appetite among Japanese clubs, academies, and federations for high-performance tools is clear, and Hudl’s ecosystem is exceptionally well-positioned to meet that demand. I look forward to working closely with organizations across the country and helping teams and individuals achieve success through Hudl’s advanced technologies.” Kengo Miura joins Hudl from LinkedIn, where he served in Global Client Sales for the Japan market. He brings over 20 years of experience from industry-leading international high-tech companies, with a proven track record of driving customer success and executing global partner programs. Kengo holds a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from Chuo University. During his tenure at both Intel and Microsoft, he resided in Shanghai and Singapore over years, where he supported a diverse global clientele and led international partner initiatives.
