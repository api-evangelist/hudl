ECNL Athletes Have More Visibility than Ever For years, the Elite Clubs National League (ECNL) has established itself as a leading pathway for players in American youth soccer. It’s built for clubs that take development seriously, for coaches who want their players seen, and for athletes who have earned the right to be found. But until today, a gap has existed between where the best American youth players competed and where college and professional coaches actually conducted their vital recruiting work. Coaches and scouts around the world use Hudl Wyscout— the global soccer database used byvirtually every NCAA program and pro clubs around the world to find and evaluate talent, build their rosters, and scout their opponents. American youth players were not consistently in it. That gap is now closed. ECNL clubs on Hudl are the first youth clubs in the nation to integrate with Wyscout, providing unparalleled visibility to scouts around the world. When a college coach is building their roster, they aren't looking at just highlight reels—they are proactively searching the Wyscout database on their own time, filtering thousands of players by statistics that match their program's specific tactical standards. With this new function, a college coach can now find an ECNL player from across the country simply by matching the necessary metrics and having full game film to prove it. This system works because it provides the connective tissue of the game that recruiters value most. While highlight reels only show a player’s best moments, coaches need to evaluate what doesn’t show up in a montage—what a player does off the ball, how they react to mistakes, and comprehensive data over the course of a season. Wyscout provides that permanent, searchable record—elevating players into the room where recruiting decisions are made. For ECNL clubs using Hudl, this powerful recruiting infrastructure is built directly into your existing workflow with no additional cost: Automatic Integration: Beginning with the 2026/27 ECNL season, coverage spans all ECNL Boys and Girls League Exchange games—regular season, showcase, playoff, and finals—for U15 through U18/U19 teams. Zero Extra Work: Coming in the 2026-27 season, video captured by your Focus camera is automatically added to the League Exchange and flows directly into Wyscout, meaning no additional steps or uploads are required. Advanced Data: While the video analysis workflow stays within Hudl, the Wyscout data is professional-grade and includes advanced metrics such as expected goals (xG), duels won, and player heatmaps, providing a meaningful edge for your club. This innovation creates more opportunities for more players, cementing ECNL's role as a premier pathway for American youth talent.
