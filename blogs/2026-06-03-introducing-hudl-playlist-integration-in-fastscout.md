Hudl has integrated its video playlist functionality directly into FastScout, eliminating the need for coaches to manage separate workflows between video libraries and scouting reports. The integration allows staff to embed video clips from Hudl playlists into specific sections of FastScout reports, with automatic updates ensuring teams always reference the most current footage. This unified system works across desktop and mobile platforms, streamlining game preparation for basketball programs using both tools.
