Expected goals (xG) is a statistical metric that calculates the probability each shot should result in a goal based on factors like position, angle, and creation method. The article explains how xG reveals whether match outcomes were deserved by comparing actual scores to the quality of chances created, helping viewers understand the underlying narrative beyond final scoreboards.
