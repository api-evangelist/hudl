Hudl has renewed its exclusive scouting rights agreement with the Oceania Football Confederation, continuing to provide access to regional men's and women's football competitions. The partnership covers eleven competitions including the OFC Champions League and various national team qualifying tournaments. Video content from these competitions will be available through Hudl Wyscout, the company's football video and data platform.
